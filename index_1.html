What is PVC laminate?

PVC laminates is a high grade material. which is made of poly-vinyl carbonate & wood fibre.

PVC laminate can be easily cut, fabricated, sawed, drilled and Paste. It also can be painted, Excellent for signs, displays & model exhibits Attractive surface is easy to clean , Durable & hard wearing under many adverse conditions Resists most chemicals & water Good sound dampening properties Meets Customer expectations.

  • Thickness: 3 mm

What is UV Coated PVC Marble?

UV Coated marble is another form of PVC laminates besides its coated with UV Protection. The UV coated marble pannel is 100% waterproof & termite proof. It comes more than 35 plus shade of marbel and wood finish. UV marble panels are ideal for wall covering products that can be used in interior.

They are great solution to ugly, stubborn problematic wall, ceiling or any surface that needs covering.
